Undergraduate and Transfer Student Programs

Expert College Guidance provide students holistic review of their overall preparedness to pursue future opportunities such as internships, scholarships, job shadowing, or even publishing uniquely generated research. Students who prepare early ensure that they are well-positioned to maximize future opportunities inside and outside of the classroom. Investing early ensures that your family is well-informed to make strategic decisions critical to developing a competitive and compelling college candidate.

College Application Assistance allows us to work one-on-one for students, helping them through the arduous, yet exciting process of preparing for college. Today’s competitive college admission environment requires more than an elite education and high test scores. Our exclusive curriculum involves interactive meetings leading toward effective academic planning and strategic engagement in extracurricular activities. Our personalized guidance delivers highly customized services that allow optimal academic planning.

College Application Boot Camp is for ambitious students wanting to get a jump start on his/her college applications the summer prior to the senior year. Our comprehensive guidance includes all aspects of admissions, including essential interview skills workshops, selecting best-fit colleges, crafting an effective personal statement, create a winning activity sheets, identifying the best teachers to ask for strong letters of recommendations, and ensuring that the student’s academic and personal accomplishments are best represented.



The Common App can be a daunting and stressful experience for all students. Our expert guidance helps students polish and complete all essays, develop a college list of safety, reach, match, and dream schools, enhance activities sheet and resumes so that student accomplishments are noticed by admissionofficers. Furthermore, our experts will walk you through how to identify, select, and approach teachers for strong letters of recommendation.

Personal Statements should strongly reflect the applicant as a strong, viable college candidate who will contribute to the overall campus intellectual community. We work with students in person, email, phone, or internet video, including Skype to address how to best structure your essays, discover your voice and tone, and of course, ensure that your style, diction, and grammar are of the highest quality.

Interview Prep Counseling can help ease those nerves and help you present yourself in the best possible way to alumni and admission officers. Highly selective colleges typically include interviews as part of the admission process and either informational or evaluative. We take the mystery and stress out of the college admission process and help you every step of the way.

Graduate/Professional School Counseling

These elite services are exclusively for students applying to professional programs such as Law school, Medical (M.D. and D.O.) schools, or graduate programs. Once you are ready to begin the application process we work with you to develop an effective application strategy, assess your experiences, inventory your best achievements, and present your academic and professional history and goals in a compelling manner.

International Students

Secure a competitive edge in the college admission process through our expertise and knowledge about American Universities. International students must navigate the complexities involved with being an international applicant. Our staff lessens the stress and mysteries behind applying to American colleges and universities. Today’s technology such as video conferencing, shared document filing systems, and highly communicative and personable consultants provide quality services to our clients. We work withour international clients seeking to identify the best target universities, decipher the Common Application, and edit personal statements and essays. We work with clients applying to undergraduate and graduate programs, and help tailor their application to the most elite American universities. We will even guide students on how to leave a favorable impression during interviews.

*Please note that all personal statements or essays must be written by the student, in order to reflect his/her personality and characteristics. We recognize the challenges associated with this type of writing. This is why our staff is here to support the student’s efforts and help him/her reach their goals.

Best-Match School Search offers families a customized approach when seeking the best opportunities for students. We realize that identifying the best school for students can be a challenge. For this reason, we have developed services to guide families through the selection process aimed at matching the best school programming with future goals. Our expert counseling services explain the nuances of school admission guidelines, and help families create a strategic plan of action and timeline. We work with families to identify their goals, analyze school options and create a personalized list of schools.
